About us

Government Engineering College, Barton Hill (GECBH), situated in the heart of the capital city of Thiruvananthapuram,Kerala is one of the premier Institutes for engineering education in Kera la. GEC Barton Hill was established by Government of Kera la in the academic year 1999-2000.Within a short span of of twenty two years from inception, the college has achieved notable academic, technical as well as career excellence.


Our mission is to Impart engineering knowledge skills and social responsibility. The college is much in demand for its synergistic learning environment and the academic rigour that it offers. Through two decades following its inception, GEC Barton Hill has emerged as a clear leader, a much respected voice among the engineering Intelligentsia.
